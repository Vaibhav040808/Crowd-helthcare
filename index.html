About Us

We are a non-profit organization that helps individuals raise funds for urgent medical treatment and surgeries. Your generous donations can help save lives and provide financial relief to people in dire need.

Mohan's Heart Surgery Fund

Due to his inability to afford the procedure, his family is reaching out for help. Every contribution will go toward covering the costs of the surgery, post-operative care, and medication.

  • Age: 45 years
